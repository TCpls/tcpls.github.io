A Little About Us

Paco's Tacos brings you a true taste of Mexico within a fun casual environment. When you first step through the door you become part of the atmosphere, joining a diverse crowd enjoying flavors picked directly from Mexico.

We are a family-founded restuarant raised within the restaurant industry. We promise to offer all guests only our highest quality ingredients and create an experience we hope you never forget.

So prepare for a treat, with the authentic Mexican flavors of Paco's Tacos. Come Hungry, Leave Happy.
